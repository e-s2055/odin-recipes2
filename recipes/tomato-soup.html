Tomato Soup

A bowl of tomato soup.

A delcious recipe for tomato soup for the winter.

Ingredients

  1. 4 C (8-10) tomatoes, peeled, cored, and chopped OR 4 C crushed tomatoes
  2. 4 C tomato juice or mixture of tomato juice and vegetable OR chicken stock
  3. 12-14 fresh basil leaves, plus more for garnish
  4. 1 C whipping cream
  5. 1 stick (1/2 C) sweet unsalted butter, softened
  6. salt to taste
  7. 1/4 cracked black pepper
  8. crusty bread
