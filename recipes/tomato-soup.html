Tomato Soup

A bowl of tomato soup.

A delcious recipe for tomato soup for the winter.

Ingredients

  • 4 C (8-10) tomatoes, peeled, cored, and chopped OR 4 C crushed tomatoes
  • 4 C tomato juice or mixture of tomato juice and vegetable OR chicken stock
  • 12-14 fresh basil leaves, plus more for garnish
  • 1 C whipping cream
  • 1 stick (1/2 C) sweet unsalted butter, softened
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 cracked black pepper
  • crusty bread

Steps

  1. Combine 4 C crushed tomatoes and juice into sauce plan
  2. Simmer over medium-low heat for 30 minutes
  3. Cool slightly and place into blender or food processor with 12-14 basil leaves
  4. Process to puree, ths will have to be done in batches. If you have an immersion stick, it will work well OR if you've used crushed tomatoes you MAY be able to skip this step
  5. Return mixture to sauce pan
  6. Add 1 C whipping cream and 1 stick unsalted butter
  7. Stir over low heat until cream and butter are incorporated
  8. Stir in salt to taste and black pepper before serving
  9. Granish with basil leaves and server with crusty bread, makes 8 servings
