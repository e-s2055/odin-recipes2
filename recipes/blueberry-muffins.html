Blueberry Muffins

This is the To Die For Blueberry Muffin Recipe

This blueberry muffin recipe is moist, sweet, and bursting with fruity flavor. A buttery streusel topping takes this indulgent breakfast over the top. This is one of my favorite blueberry muffin recipes I've tried!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 C all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 C white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 C vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 C milk
  • 1 C fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 C white sugar
  • 1/3 C all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 C butter, cubed
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
