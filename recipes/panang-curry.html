Panang Curry

A warm bowl of panang curry.

An easy meal that tastes complex. One of my favorite recipes to cook!

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp Panang Curry Paste
  • Cooking Oil
  • 4 C coconut milk
  • 2/3 LB skinless, boneless chicken breast, cubed
  • 2 tbsp palm sugar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 6 keffir lime leaves
  • 2 fresh red chili peppers, sliced
  • 1/4 C fresh thai basil leaves, or 6 tsp dried
  • 1 LB fresh veggies to taste
