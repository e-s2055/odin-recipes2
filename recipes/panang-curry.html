Panang Curry

A warm bowl of panang curry.

An easy meal that tastes complex. One of my favorite recipes to cook!

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp Panang Curry Paste
  • Cooking Oil
  • 4 C coconut milk
  • 2/3 LB skinless, boneless chicken breast, cubed
  • 2 tbsp palm sugar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 6 keffir lime leaves
  • 2 fresh red chili peppers, sliced
  • 1/4 C fresh thai basil leaves, or 6 tsp dried
  • 1 LB fresh veggies to taste, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or pan
  2. Stir fry chopped fresh veggies of choice until slightly tender
  3. Add 5 tbsp panang curry paste until fragrant
  4. Stir 4 C coconut milk in and bring to a boil
  5. Add 2/3 LB cubed chicken until it is nearly cooked through, around 10 to 15 minutes
  6. Stir in palm sugar, fish sauce, lime leaves or lime zest, basil leaves, and red chile peppers into mixture
  7. Simmer together for 5 minutes
  8. Serve with fresh white rice or rice noodles
